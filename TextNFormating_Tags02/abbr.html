ABBREVIATION Tag

Details:

 * The <abbr>tag is used to define an abbreviation or acronym. When you hover over it, the full meaning (provided with the title attribute) appears as a tooltip.

Syntax of ABBREVIATION Tag

<abbr title="Full Meaning">Abbreviation</abbr>
