INS Tag

Details:

 * The <ins>tag is used to mark inserted or added text in an HTML document.
* By default, it is shown with an underline, often used together with <del> to show content updates or revisions.

Syntax of INS Tag

<ins>Inserted text</ins>

Example of INS Tag

        * html:
    <!DOCTYPE html>
        <html>
        <head>
            <title>INS Tag Example</title>
        </head>
        <body>
            <p>The meeting has been rescheduled from <del>Monday</del> to <ins>Wednesday</in>.</p>
        </body>
        </html>

            The meeting has been rescheduled from Monday to Wednesday.
