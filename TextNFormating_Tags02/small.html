SMALL Tag

Details:

* The <small>tag is used to display smaller-sized text compared to the surrounding text.
* It is often used for fine print, disclaimers, copyright notes, etc.

Syntax of SMALL Tag

<small>Smaller text</small>

Example of SMALL Tag

    <!DOCTYPE html>
        <html>
        <head>
            <title>SMALL Tag Example</title>
        </head>
        <body>
            <p>This is a normal sentence. This part is in small text.</p>
        </body>
        </html>
