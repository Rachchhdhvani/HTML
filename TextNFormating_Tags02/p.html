P Tag

Details:

 * The <p> tag is used to define a paragraph of text in HTML. It automatically adds some space (margin) before and after the paragraph.

Syntax of P Tag

<p>This is a paragraph of text.</p>

Example of P Tag

        <p>This is a <strong>bold</strong> word inside a paragraph.</p>
        <p>Visit <a href="https://example.com">this link</a> for more info.</p>
