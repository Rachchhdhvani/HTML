HR Tag

Details:

 * The <hr> tag stands for Horizontal Rule. It is used to insert a horizontal line (divider) across the webpage. It's mainly used to separate sections of content.

Syntax of HR Tag

<p>This is the first line.<hr>This is the second line.</p>

Example of HR Tag

        
            <html>
            <head>
                <title>HR Tag Example</title>
            </head>
            <body>
                <h1>My Blog</h1>
                <p>This is the first section of my blog.</p>

                


                <p>This is the second section, after a horizontal line.</p>
            </body>
            </html>
